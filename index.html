Vibe Musical

What is Vibe Musical?


Vibe Musical, a brand new app that teaches you all about music. We log instruments, and gather their history. Once you find an instrument that you connect with we give you access to tutorials to learn how to play it. If you're feeling creative try checking out our create magic section where you can get to play some interactive musical instruments . We look to the future to develop new ways to make learning about music and instruments both easy and fun.
